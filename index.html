Our Product

LegAI, a start-up providing an Ai-dvisor that combines Artificial Intelligence and Law to make data protection compliance accessible to SMEs and start-ups. We aim for GDPR compliance by guiding our clients through legislation in an easy-to-understand and accurate way. With a business model designed around reliability, time and cost efficiency as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product, our Ai-dvisor interacts with the client to understand the business profile and requirements. An assessment report is then generated with the identified risks containing the necessary advice.

Our Story

History in a nutshell.

  • 1956

    Ai

    Artificial intelligence (AI) is founded as a research field. AI is the ability of a computer or a robot controlled by a computer to do tasks that are usually done by humans because they require human intelligence and discernment.

  • 1980

    XCON

    XCON becomes the first industrial expert system to win success. XCON is a Rule-based system written in OPS5 in 1978 to assist in the ordering of DEC's VAX computer systems by automatically selecting the computer system components based on the customer's requirements.

  • 2002

    The four colors theorem

    The four colors theorem is proved using the Coq proof assistance. The 150 year old Four Colour Theorem (every map drawn on the plane be coloured with four colours so that no two regions having a common border have the same colour), is the first famous result with a proof that required the Coq proof assistant, to write an extended program that specifies both the calculations and their mathematical justification.

  • 2019

    Normative reasoning

    A paper by Tomer Libal (our CEO) and Matteo (TBD) about Normative Reasoning is presented in the International Conference on Artifical Intelligence and Law.

  • 2020

    NAI editor

    Tomer Libal's and Alex (TBD)'s NAI editor is created, and wins first prize at the IRIS Conference.

  • 2021

    Funding

    The LegAi project wins the Luxembourgish FNR PoC grant.

  • 2022

    The LegAi solution

    The Ai-dvisor, that combines Artificial Intelligence and Law, is presented.
