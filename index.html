Ai-dvisor

LegAi, a start-up providing an Ai-dvisor that combines Artificial Intelligence and Law to make data protection compliance accessible to small businesses and start-ups. We aim for GDPR compliance by guiding our clients through legislation in an easy-to-understand and accurate way. With a business model designed around reliability, time and cost efficiency as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) product, our Ai-dvisor interacts with the client to understand the business profile and requirements. An assessment report is then generated with the identified risks containing the necessary advice. Our start-up is supported by the University of Luxembourg.
